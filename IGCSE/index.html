About IGCSE Notes

A student-led initiative. Here at IGCSE Notes, we provide a multitude of resources for you to learn, understand and achieve that A* in your IGCSE's

The founder of IGCSE Notes is Shaun. He has gotten 9 A*'s in the 9 subjects he took while he was in IGCSE. He is currently in IBDP and working towards a Computer Science Degree

Our Vision
