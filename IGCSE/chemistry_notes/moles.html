● Avogadro's number
○ Equal volume of gases, at the same temperature and pressure, contain equal number of
particles
● The molar volume of a gas is the volume occupied by 1 mole of the gas
● At RTP (room temperature) which is 25 degree and 1 atm of gas has a volume of 24dm3 or
24000cm3
● Number of moles = Volume of gas/ RTP (24)
● Volume of gas = 24 x number of moles
● Concentration
○ A concentration of 1 mole per dm3 is written as 1 mole/dm3
○ Concentration = mass/volume of (gas/solute)
○ Concentration = moles/volume of (gas/solute)
○ Moles = concentration x volume
